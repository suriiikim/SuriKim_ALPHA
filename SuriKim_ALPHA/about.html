ABOUT

Hey, I am Suri Kim, a web developer, UX/UI designer, specialized in visual designs. Currently based in Valencia, Spain.

Within the last couple of years, I have focused my work on multidisciplinary visual arts. My artistic work is based on investigations about how technology affects the way we interact with each other. Some of the work consists of interactive installations which uses coding or audiovisual mediums like videos.

My interests in technology and codes lead me study Frontend coding (html/css/javascript), UX/UI design, and marketing to persue my career as a coder/designer.
